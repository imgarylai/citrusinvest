## What this is

**citrusinvest** turns a one-line strategy into an equity curve, a trade list,
and a full metrics report. Two pieces:

- **lemon** — a small, readable DSL. `is_largest(sma(close, 2), 3)` means
  *"hold the three names with the highest 2-day average close."*
- **yuzu** — a pure, I/O-free engine that evaluates the strategy against your
  price/fundamental panels and produces a `Report`.

It compiles to native (batch backtests via Rayon) **and** WebAssembly — which is
exactly why the [playground](playground) can run a real backtest with no server.
